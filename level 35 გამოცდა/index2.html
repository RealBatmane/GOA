TIPS & CULTURE

GEORGIAN FOOD

Georgian cuisine is very popular among tourists. Famous dishes include Khachapuri (cheese bread), Khinkali (dumplings), and Mtsvadi (grilled meat). Georgian food is tasty and filling.

Traditions and Culture

Georgia has a rich culture and long history. Traditional music, dancing, and feasts called “supra” are important parts of Georgian life. Hospitality is a key value in Georgian culture.

Transpot Tips

Public transport in Georgia is cheap and easy to use. In cities, you can use buses and metro. For traveling between cities, minibuses called “marshrutka” are very common.

Safety Tips

Georgia is considered a safe country for tourists. Still, it is important to be careful with personal belongings and follow basic safety rules, especially in crowded places.

Budget Tips

Traveling in Georgia is affordable. Food, transport, and accommodation are cheaper compared to many European countries, which makes Georgia a great destination for budget travelers.
