LOCATIONS

TBILISI

Tbilisi is the capital city of Georgia. It is famous for its old town, colorful houses, and historical buildings. Tourists can visit Narikala Fortress, walk on Rustaveli Avenue, and enjoy sulfur baths.

BATUMI

Batumi is a seaside city located on the Black Sea coast. It is popular for its beaches, modern buildings, and nightlife. Batumi Boulevard is a great place for walking and relaxing.

Kazbegi

Kazbegi is a mountain region known for its stunning views. The Gergeti Trinity Church is one of the most famous landmarks in Georgia. This place is perfect for hiking and nature lovers.

Kutaisi

Kutaisi is one of the oldest cities in Georgia. It is known for historical monuments such as Bagrati Cathedral and Gelati Monastery. Nearby caves like Prometheus Cave attract many tourists.

Borjomi

Borjomi is famous for its mineral water and green parks. Many people visit Borjomi to relax and enjoy fresh air. Borjomi National Park is great for walking and nature trips.

Svaneti

Svaneti is a high mountain region known for its medieval towers and strong traditions. Mestia is the main town of Svaneti and a popular destination for adventure tourism.
